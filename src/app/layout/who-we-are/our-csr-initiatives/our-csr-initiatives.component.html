Our CSR Initiatives

“We make a living by what we get, but we make a life by what we give.”

-Winston Churchill

NeoSOFT is built and grown by the combination of achievements, business expansion and commitment to the society. Moving beyond the traditional bottom line, we adopt the triple bottom line method to see our business as a social, environmental and financial entity. Under the aegis of NeoSOFT FOUNDATION, we actively contribute towards the development of humankind and the society we live in. The objective is to build a better and sustainable livelihood for the weaker and impoverished sections of the society.

Our Initiatives

Child education

Education remains the backbone of every progressive society in this world, and many are still deprived of quality education. Providing education opportunities to the needy is the core CSR mission of NeoSOFT and while doing so, we aim at helping the aspiring students in the best possible manner.

We have contributed towards formal and non-formal education, adult education, scholarships for girls, merit scholarships, technical education, distance education, girl child education, and digital literacy/computer education.

Employability

While unemployment can be the biggest worry for the youths today, we aim at creating a solution to address the root-cause of this problem. With opportunities in abundance, the need of the hour is to develop employability skills and be able to crack the chance. We look at all the dimensions of a candidate while providing employment opportunity. Employment opportunities for the destitute, skill development and vocational training are some of the initiatives we undertake to boost the employability factor of the needy.

Medical Assistance

We commit ourselves towards the health and safety of everyone. For the impoverished who cannot afford medical expenses, NeoSOFT grants financial assistance for their medical treatment. This could include treatment of fatal health issues, surgeries, therapies or regular treatment or life-threatening diseases.

Environment Protection

We have adopted the approach of responsibility towards the prevention and alleviation of environmental damage. We take extra care to maintain the environment which is an utmost part of our ecosystem. Our CSR encompasses programs and drives to create awareness on environment protection, conduct plantation drives, water conservation programs, organic farming, cleanliness, and other awareness drives.

Disaster Relief

Over the past few years, we have been facing significant natural disasters, uprooting the lives of many and leaving several unsheltered and homeless. For every natural calamity that occurs, we allocate funds from our disaster relief quota and also work towards pooling helping hands from other possible sources.

Women Empowerment

We honour the power of women and try our best to empower women in various ways to sustain herself in different walks of life. Our CSR initiatives aim at educating a girl child, encouraging the entrepreneurial spirit of women, economic empowerment, and help in creating employment opportunities for the needy. We also support them with financial assistance by way of loans and grants.

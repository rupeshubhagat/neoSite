ODC / BOT

Dedicated, On-Demand, Secure & Customized – which follow your Practices, Processes, Methodologies & Culture

Offshore Development Center (ODC) is preferred approach for Enterprises, SMEs & Startups all over the globe to systematically implement, maintain & enhance software products for their business. ODC acts like an arm of their organization which leverages the rich & proven reserves of IT Human Capital & Low-Cost advantage of India. We help them set up Dedicated, On-Demand, Secure & Customized software offshore development centers in India for Enterprises, SMEs & Startups - which follow their Practices, Processes, Methodologies & Culture. A budget is pre-defined by the client which is strictly followed by us while hiring resources, building ODC.

There are 3 main components of ODC:

  • Build- Hire Resources, Setup Infrastructure & Development Environments, Establish Practices / Processes / Methodologies / Culture
  • Operate- Training & Development, Measure Efficiency, Knowledge & Intellectual Property (IP) Protection, Regulation & Compliance
  • Transfer (if required)-Entire operational company to client based on pre-defined T&C and milestones achievement.

Advantages of ODC

  • 300% Lower Cost
  • 100% Transparent Operation
  • Multi Technology Team
  • Intellectual Property Protection
  • Ramp Up & Ramp Down as per need
